About Us

DHT Cosmetic and Hair Transplant Centre

made a humble beginning on 15 June 2019 at Shopprix Mall, Meerut (U.P.) after serving Delhi-NCR for almost 5 years. DHT Centre is one of the most prominent names in Meerut for hair transplant and cosmetic surgery started by Dr. Arshad Ali Siddiqui.

Dr. Arshad Ali Siddiqui is a renowned expert in hair transplant and related cosmetic procedures having a collective experience of more than five yrs in hair transplant and a clinical experience of more than fifteen yrs. Roots of his expertise and his fine touch for hair & cosmetic transplants are grounded from his ultimate mentors like Dr. Ashit Gupta (M.Ch. in Plastic Surgergy). His quality of work is the best judge which is due to his sheer dedication and perfection in his delivery.

Within a short span of 5-6 yrs, he made a debut by establishing himself as a go to person for Hair Transplant Surgery in entire Delhi-NCR region.
